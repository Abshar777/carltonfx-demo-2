Price Feed Discrepancy Policy

General

At Carlton, we are committed to maintaining fairness and transparency in all trading activities. In the rare event of a price feed discrepancy caused by technical issues, we ensure that corrective measures are taken promptly to address any affected trades.

How We Handle Price Feed Errors

  • Trades impacted by incorrect price feeds will be reviewed and adjusted accordingly.
  • Any profits or losses resulting from the incorrect feed will be recalculated and adjusted in the respective trading accounts to reflect the correct market conditions.
  • Clients will be notified about the adjustments made and the reasons behind them.
