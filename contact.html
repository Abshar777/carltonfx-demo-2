Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Carlton FX?

    Carlton FX is a trusted online trading platform that gives you access to Forex, Stocks, Commodities, Indices, and Cryptocurrencies—all in one secure and user-friendly app.

  • Is Carlton FX regulated and secure?

    Yes. At Carlton FX, we prioritize your safety. All transactions are encrypted, accounts are secured with multi-factor authentication, and trading is conducted under strict compliance standards.

  • What types of accounts can I open?

    Carlton FX offers multiple account types tailored to beginners, intermediate traders, and professionals. Each comes with competitive spreads, leverage options, and exclusive trading tools.

  • How do I get started with Carlton FX?

    Click on Sign Up, complete the registration form, verify your identity (KYC), and fund your account. Once approved, you can start trading immediately.

  • What markets can I trade on Carlton FX?

    You can trade Forex pairs, Global Stocks, Commodities (like Gold & Oil), Major Indices, and Cryptocurrencies.

  • What deposit and withdrawal methods are available?

    We support secure payments via bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and leading e-wallets. Withdrawals are processed quickly, ensuring fast access to your funds.

  • Does Carlton FX offer a demo account?

    Yes. You can open a free demo account with virtual funds to practice your trading skills before moving to a live account.

  • What is the minimum deposit at Carlton FX?

    The minimum deposit starts from just $50, making it easy for new traders to begin their journey.

  • Can I trade on mobile?

    Yes. The Carlton FX app is available on both iOS and Android, giving you the flexibility to trade anytime, anywhere.
